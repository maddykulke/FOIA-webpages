What type of medicare beneficiary claims are you requesting?
Does the beneficiary have a Medicare card?
Not Entrolled in Medicare

If the beneficiary does not have a Medicare card they are not enrolled and therefore records are not maintained by CMS. Contact the correct insurer.

Does the beneficiary have Medicare Advantage?
Medicare advantage

CMS does not maintain Medicare Advantage claims records. You will need to submit your request directly to the specic private insurance company. (e.g. HMO, PPO, etc.)

Does the beneficiary have Medicaid?
Medicaid

CMS does not maintain Medicaid claims records. You will need to submit your request directly to the state agency. State contact information:
